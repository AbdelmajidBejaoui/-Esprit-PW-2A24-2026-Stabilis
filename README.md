# Stabilis

## Overview
This project was developed as part of the **PW – 2nd Year Engineering Program** at
**Esprit School of Engineering – Tunisia** (Academic Year 2025–2026).

**Stabilis** is an integrated digital platform designed for the modern athlete who
refuses to choose between physical performance and the health of the planet.
The goal is to transform sports nutrition — often associated with overconsumption
of processed and packaged products — into a lever for sustainable performance.

By directly connecting local organic farmers with urban consumers and athletes,
Stabilis eliminates intermediaries to reduce carbon footprint, promote sustainable
agriculture, and provide fresh, organic products at fair prices.

## Sustainable Nutritional Performance Platform

Stabilis is an integrated digital platform designed for the modern athlete who
refuses to choose between physical performance and the health of the planet.
The goal is to transform sports nutrition, often associated with overconsumption
of processed and packaged products, into a lever for sustainable performance.

## Features
- **Authentication** – Sign up / Login for athletes and users
- **Product Listings** – Browse organic and natural sports nutrition products
- **Product Catalog** – Filter by category, nutritional value, and price
- **Shopping Cart** – Add products to cart and place orders
- **Order Management** – Track and manage your orders
- **Training & Challenges** – Access training plans, exercises and challenges
- **Nutrition Tracking** – Track meals, recipes and daily food intake
- **Leaderboard & Progress** – Follow your performance and compare with others
- **Search & Filters** – Search by name, category or nutritional content
